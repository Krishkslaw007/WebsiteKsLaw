About the Advocate

Adv. Krishan Swami

Whether you are facing life-altering criminal charges or the delicate transition of a divorce, our firm stands on a foundation of trial-tested litigation and meticulous strategy. We don't just represent clients; we safeguard their rights, their reputations, and their families.

Adv. Krishan Swami is a premier legal practitioner at KS and Law Associates, recognized for a relentless approach to justice. Enrolled with the Bar Council of Rajasthan and practising across the Rajasthan High Court and district courts in Jaipur, Adv. Swami brings a meticulous, client-first approach to every case.

Beyond the courtroom, he is an active voice in community legal awareness, regularly conducting free legal camps across Jaipur to educate citizens on their rights under Hindu, Muslim, and Special Marriage Acts.

He accepts matters in various High Courts across India, particularly in neighboring states, for complex litigation requiring a specialized Jaipur-based counsel. He also frequently provides counsel at the District & Sessions Court (Jaipur).

Practice Areas

Legal Expertise

Dual expertise in Criminal Defense and Matrimonial Law — delivered with the precision, strategy, and sensitivity each case demands.

Divorce Consultation

Expert guidance on mutual consent and contested divorce under Hindu Marriage Act, Special Marriage Act, and personal laws. Clear roadmaps for every stage of the process.

Cyber Crime

Strategic action for stopping Investigation Agency Orders and Freezes, recovering lost funds through tracking and chargebacks, and expertly drafting comprehensive FIRs and cyber complaints.

Criminal Defense

Aggressive defense against everything from white-collar crimes to serious felonies, ensuring that the presumption of innocence is never just a formality. Your freedom is our mandate.

NDPS Cases

Strategic legal counsel and defense in matters under the Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances (NDPS) Act, offering expert examination of search, seizure, and procedural compliance.

Alimony & Maintenance

Determination, negotiation, and enforcement of spousal maintenance and alimony claims, ensuring fair financial settlements for both parties.

Child Custody & Support

Protecting the best interests of children in custody disputes. Legal assistance in visitation rights, guardianship, and maintenance for minor children.

Property Division

Legal counsel on equitable distribution of matrimonial assets — immovable property, joint investments, business interests, and inherited wealth.

Domestic Violence

Urgent legal protection under the Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act. Filing protection orders, residence orders, and monetary relief applications.

Marital Settlement

Structured settlement agreements for amicable resolution of matrimonial disputes — saving time, cost, and emotional strain through skilled negotiation.
